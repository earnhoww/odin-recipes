Yummy Sweet Potato Casserole

This baked sweet potato casserole topped with a delicious pecan topping is so good and creamy â€” my family begs me to make it every Thanksgiving and Christmas! Try this recipe and I'm sure it will become your new tradition.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups peeled, cubed sweet potatoes
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ vanilla extract
  • 4 tablesspoons butter, softened

Steps

  1. Cook and mash the sweet potatoes.
  2. Mix the casserole ingredients and transfer to a baking dish.
  3. Make the topping and sprinkle it over the sweet potato mixture.
  4. Bake until the topping is lightly browned.
